# Visual Bible — TARS

**Core Metaphor**: Elegant orbital monolith (tall, precise, slightly tapered black rectangular prism with subtle technical panel lines and data etchings) floating in deep space with delicate orbital geometry. The calm, competent AI companion on a long-duration mission.

**Emotional Promise**: Quiet, immense competence. The feeling of a reliable, high-precision partner for deep work and exploration. Long-voyage reliability. Calm power.

**Visual DNA Keywords**: Asiimov technical, aerospace precision, deep space void, orbital mechanics, monolith, clean linework, restrained warmth, Interstellar/Expanse aesthetic, quiet authority, data etchings, starfield discipline.

**Color System**:
- Deep void: #050308, #0a0c14, #11151f
- Technical cold white / light gray: #f0f4f8, #e8eef5, #c5ced9
- Cold steel: #8a96a3, #5f6b78
- Restrained warm accent (only for precise highlights): #b85a42 or #c85a3f (never dominant, never red)
- No neon, no cyberpunk purple/teal

**Typography Direction**: Modern grotesque (Inter spirit or similar), slightly condensed for technical feel, generous letter-spacing on "TARS", crisp and authoritative. Small supporting text in refined lighter weight. Never decorative or rounded.

**Audience & Cultural Notes**: Technical power users, AI builders, sci-fi literature/film fans (2001, Interstellar, The Expanse). Brazilian creator with sophisticated taste. They value tools that feel engineered for serious long-term use.

**Forbidden Styles**:
- Anything red or Sharingan/Mangekyō (that is Kamui's identity)
- Anime, manga, or Japanese pop visual tropes
- Heavy glows, lens flares, busy particles
- Corporate blue gradients, generic tech spheres
- Cute, playful, or overly colorful treatments
- Low-contrast or muddy renders

## Reference Assets (Approved Direction — 2026-05-31)

**Primary Horizontal Logo (first generation)**:
- Monolith positioned with strong presence on left/integration with wordmark
- "TARS" in crisp technical white with excellent tracking
- Deep space with natural star variation + faint cold nebulae
- One or two razor-precise warm accent lines only
- Quiet, confident, aerospace-grade

**App Icon / Square Mark (second generation)**:
- Monolith as pure symbol, perfectly readable down to 16px
- Deep space background, subtle orbital arc
- Exceptional precision in panel lines and highlights
- No text — the monolith alone carries the brand

**Hero / Social Banner (third generation)**:
- Cinematic wide composition with strong negative space
- Monolith(s) in lower or left third
- Integrated elegant typography treatment
- Vast, professional, awe-inspiring but restrained

All future generations for TARS must reference and stay extremely faithful to this monolith + Asiimov deep space language. When in doubt, make it quieter and more precise rather than more dramatic.
