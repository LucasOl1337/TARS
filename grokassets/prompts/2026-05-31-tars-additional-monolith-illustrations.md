# Prompt — TARS Additional Deep Space Monolith Illustrations

**Project**: TARS
**Asset Type**: Feature illustrations / content
**Priority**: High for brand depth

## Visual Bible Reference
Elegant orbital monolith in deep space. Asiimov technical + Interstellar/Expanse aesthetic. Quiet authority, restrained warmth only for precise highlights. Deep void blacks and cold technical grays. No neon, no busy particles, no lens flares.

## Exact Prompt
Multiple refined compositions of the TARS monolith in deep space for feature illustrations. One version: the monolith perfectly centered with delicate orbital geometry lines and very faint cold nebulae. Another: the monolith in extreme lower third with vast negative space and a single precise warm accent line catching light. Third: two monoliths in quiet conversation across a vast distance, with perfect aerospace precision in panel lines and data etchings. All in the exact TARS visual language — calm, immense competence, long-voyage reliability. Extremely high production value, minimal, authoritative.
